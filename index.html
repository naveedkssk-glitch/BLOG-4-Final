RDA Cracks Down on Illegal Housing Schemes in Rawalpindi

By Sher Alam Published Sep 25, 2025 2:32 pm

The Rawalpindi Development Authority (RDA) has intensified its crackdown on illegal housing societies and unauthorized constructions following the directives of its Director General, Kinza Murtaza.

In a recent operation, the RDA Task Force sealed the site office of an unlawful housing project and registered a First Information Report (FIR) against its owners. An RDA spokesperson confirmed that the drive is being conducted under the supervision of DG Kinza Murtaza, who has stressed that strict measures will continue to protect citizens from fraud and land grabbing carried out in the name of illegal housing schemes.
